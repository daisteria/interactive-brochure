Post-tire change Checklist

A checklist of maintenance after tire change.

Make sure to put away all equipment back into the trunk of

the vehicle for future use. If a flat tire was replaced, then

take the flat tire to the tire service center as soon

as possible.

Follow your vehicle's car owner manual for the specific air pressure for a tire.

If you do not have access to it, you can set the air pressure to 60 psi for spare tires. For

seasonal tire air pressure, you can find the air pressure of the old, replaced tires and set the new tires to that psi.
